Our Classes

Discover the perfect dance style for you and check our weekly schedule

Dance Classes

Beginner

Zumba Fitness

Mayur Arora 60 min

A high-energy Latin dance workout that combines all the rhythms in Zumba. Perfect for beginners looking for a fun way to get fit.

Intermediate

Zumba Toning

Mayur Arora 60 min

Perfect for those who want to party but put extra emphasis on toning and sculpting to define muscles. Uses Zumba Toning Sticks.

Beginner

Hip-Hop Basics

Tarun Arora 75 min

Learn the fundamentals of hip-hop dance including popping, locking, and breaking. No experience necessary.

Advanced

Urban Choreography

Tarun Arora 90 min

Advanced hip-hop choreography class focusing on complex routines, musicality, and performance quality.

Beginner

Classical Introduction

Tarun Arora 60 min

An introduction to Indian classical dance forms with a focus on basic postures, hand gestures, and expressions.

Intermediate

Fusion Flow

Tarun Arora 75 min

Blend classical Indian dance with contemporary movements for a unique fusion experience. Some dance experience recommended.

Beginner

Freestyle Basics

Tarun Arora 60 min

Learn to express yourself through movement with this introduction to freestyle dance. Focus on improvisation and finding your own style.

Advanced

Freestyle Advanced

Tarun Arora 90 min

Advanced freestyle class focusing on developing your unique style, musicality, and performance skills. Prior dance experience required.
