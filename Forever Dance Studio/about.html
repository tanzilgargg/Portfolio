About Us

Learn about our journey, mission, and the passionate team behind Forever Dance

Our Story

Founded in 2013, Forever Dance Fitness Studio began as a small passion project in Shahabad Markanda. What started as a single room with just 5 students has now grown into Haryana's premier dance destination with over 500 students and multiple specialized training spaces.

Our journey has been one of continuous growth, learning, and adaptation. We've evolved from teaching just one style of dance to offering a comprehensive range of dance forms that cater to all ages, abilities, and interests.

Founder's Vision

Tarun Arora, our founder and lead choreographer, established Forever Dance with a clear vision: to create a space where dance is not just taught as a skill but celebrated as a way of life.

With over 15 years of professional dance experience and training from renowned institutions in Mumbai and Delhi, Tarun brings technical expertise and creative innovation to every class. His philosophy is simple: "Dance is the hidden language of the soul - we're here to help you speak it fluently."

Our Space

Our facility features a professional dance studio, wall-to-wall mirrors, and state-of-the-art sound systems. Each studio is designed to provide the optimal environment for different dance styles.

We've created a space that's both functional for serious training and welcoming for beginners. Our facilities include changing rooms, a student lounge, and a small caf√© where dancers can refuel and socialize before or after classes.

Our Philosophy

At Forever Dance, we believe that dance is for everyone. Our inclusive approach ensures that regardless of age, background, or experience level, every student finds their rhythm and builds confidence through movement.

We emphasize not just technical proficiency but also creative expression, physical fitness, and mental well-being. Our teaching methodology balances discipline with joy, tradition with innovation, and technique with self-expression.
