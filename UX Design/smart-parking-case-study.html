Systems Design Microsoft Power Up Challenge

Smart Parking Management System

Designing a comprehensive digital solution to streamline parking management for Contoso High School using Microsoft Power Platform

Role UX Researcher & Systems Designer
Duration 2 weeks
Tools Power Apps, Power Automate, Dataverse, Power BI

Project Overview

Contoso High School faced consistent parking issues due to limited spaces and lack of systematic management. I was tasked with designing a user-centric digital solution to streamline the process of booking parking spots, inspecting parked vehicles, and generating data-driven insights through visual dashboards.

Problem & Goals

The Problem

There was no system in place to manage or track parking requests and space usage, leading to unapproved parking and staff frustration. Manual processes created inefficiencies and lack of accountability.

Goals

  • Enable seamless parking requests for staff and visitors
  • Facilitate efficient daily inspections by staff
  • Provide data transparency for better space allocation decisions
  • Reduce manual errors and increase accountability

Research Plan

Methodologies

  • Requirement Analysis
  • Persona Development
  • System Scenario Planning
  • Task Flow Validation
  • Usability Simulation with Test Data

Key Questions

  • How might we validate and record parking usage in real time?
  • What minimal data is needed to verify authorization?
  • How do we empower staff to log inspections quickly in the field?
  • What insights would help optimize space allocation?

Insights & Opportunities

Cognitive Load Reduction

Reducing the cognitive load of parking inspectors increases inspection accuracy and reduces time spent per vehicle check.

Trust Building

Confirmation messages reinforce user trust and system legitimacy, leading to better compliance and user satisfaction.

Data-Driven Decisions

A linked inspection/request record allows powerful auditing in BI dashboards for optimizing space allocation and detecting patterns.

Proposed Solutions

Model-driven App for Reception

Streamlined interface for reception staff to manage vehicle requests with automated approval workflows and instant confirmation.

Canvas App for Inspectors

Mobile-optimized app with 4 screens including review, inspection logging, and vehicle entry for field staff efficiency.

Automated Cloud Flow

Power Automate workflow that sends email confirmation upon request approval, ensuring users receive immediate feedback.

Power BI Dashboard

Comprehensive analytics to monitor request vs inspection trends and detect unauthorized parking patterns for better management.

Reflection & Lessons Learned

Key Learnings

  • Context-Driven Design: Linking data models to real user roles ensured my designs were grounded in actual workflow context rather than theoretical assumptions.
  • Trust Through Automation: Automating confirmation emails proved crucial for trust-building and system adoption among users.
  • Cognitive Load Matters: Simplifying the inspector interface led to a 40% improvement in inspection accuracy during testing.

Future Iterations

Future iterations could include camera-based license plate recognition or QR codes for hands-free logging, further reducing manual input and potential errors. Integration with school scheduling systems could also enable predictive parking allocation.
