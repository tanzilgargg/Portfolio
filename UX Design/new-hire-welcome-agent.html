New Hire Welcome Agent

Autonomous Agents Challenge - Designing AI-powered onboarding automation to enhance employee experience

My Role

UX Researcher & Autonomous Agent Designer

Duration

1 Day

Tools Used

Microsoft Power Automate, Dataverse, Logic Flows

Project Overview

As part of the Autonomous Agents Challenge in the Microsoft Power Up Program, I designed and implemented an AI-powered agent that automates onboarding communication. The agent detects new hires in a company database and sends them a personalized welcome message, while also assigning them a buddy or manager for support.

The aim was to explore how autonomous systems can enhance employee experience without manual intervention ‚Äî and how structured onboarding flows can be translated into logic-driven automation.

Problem + Goals

New hires often report feeling disoriented in their first few days due to gaps in communication and lack of structured onboarding. HR teams are stretched thin and may not send welcome messages or assign onboarding support in a timely manner.
