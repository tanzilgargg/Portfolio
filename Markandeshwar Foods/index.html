About MFAPL

Our Vision

As vision, determination and expertise came together MFAPL was born. Driven by the passion of providing quality dairy products, MFAPL collects large quantities of fresh milk from farmers directly spread over northern states, using hygienic methods. We have consistently redefined our frontiers to evolve into one of the leading manufacturers, exporters and importers of dairy products in India.

Our Mission

To emerge as a strong global player in international trade capitalizing on our potential of providing quality products and achieving customer satisfaction. To be a single window company from enquiry to supply setting uncompromising standards in quality delivery schedule and speed.

Global Reach

In synergy with expanding global markets MFAPL has already taken rapid strides, with MURLI, MADHUSAGAR, LORD KRISHNA & GOODDAY brands dairy products reaching and serving the Indian/global markets with excellence.

Global Technology For Global Needs

Strategic Locations

Our office is strategically located only 30 kms from international airport New Delhi. MFAPL plant is located in holy land of LORD KRISHNA at KURUKSHETRA, 166 KMs from New Delhi on national highway, with additional plants in Malekotla (Punjab) and Palwal (Haryana).

Modern Facilities

The plant is equipped with most modern processing facilities with state-of-art laboratories to test genetic material and manufacturing process in hygienic conditions ensuring total quality management. Our plant is certified by ISI standard grade.

Rich Milk Belt

Being located in the richest milk belt of India, our location ensures availability of quality milk in abundance directly from farmers spread over northern states using hygienic collection methods.
