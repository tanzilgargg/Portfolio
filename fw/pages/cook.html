Butter Chicken

Key Ingredient

Tomatoes

Fresh, ripe tomatoes form the foundation of the rich, creamy sauce in Butter Chicken. They provide the perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess, while also contributing to the vibrant orange-red color of the dish. When cooked down with spices and cream, they create the signature velvety texture that makes this dish so beloved.

Recipe

Prep: 30min
Cook: 45min
Marination: 2h
Total Time: 3h 15min

INGREDIENTS

4-6 Servings

For the Chicken Marinade

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into chunks
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp salt

For the Makhani Gravy

  • 2 tbsp butter (unsalted)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 3 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • Extra butter for garnish
  • Fresh coriander leaves

PREPARATIONS

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl. Add chicken pieces and coat well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken

Grill, pan-fry, or bake the marinated chicken until slightly charred and cooked through. Set aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Gravy

In a pan, heat butter and oil. Sauté chopped onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates. Stir in spices and salt. Add grilled chicken pieces. Mix and cook for 5–10 minutes. Stir in cream and kasuri methi. Simmer for a few minutes.

Step 4: Serve

Garnish with fresh coriander and a swirl of cream or butter. Serve hot with naan or rice.
